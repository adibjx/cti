Common questions

Quick answers.

How much does a tattoo cost? +

Pricing depends on size, detail, placement, and the artist. Small pieces start around $150. We give you a flat quote at the consultation — never an open-ended hourly surprise.

Do I need to book in advance? +

For a specific artist or larger piece, yes. We typically book two to four weeks out. Walk-ins are welcome Monday–Saturday for smaller pieces and flash work, depending on availability.

Can I bring my own design? +

Absolutely. Bring references, sketches, or a Pinterest board. Your artist will adapt the idea to a tattoo that holds up over decades — clean lines, smart placement, age-proof.

Do you do cover-ups? +

Yes. Send a clear photo of the existing tattoo through the form below and we'll tell you honestly whether a cover-up is the right call or if laser fading first will give you a better result.

How long does a session take? +

Most pieces fit in a single 2–4 hour session. Larger work is split across multiple sittings — your artist will map this out at the consultation so you know the full timeline upfront.

What does aftercare look like? +

You'll leave with a written care sheet, the right ointment, and direct contact with your artist for the first 30 days.
